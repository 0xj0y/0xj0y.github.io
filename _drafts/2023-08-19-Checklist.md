- [ ] An impulsive move already happened of minimum 30-40 points.
- [ ] Order Block and fvgs are drawn.

- [ ] Price didn't reach zones very fast.
- [ ] Reversal candlestick formed.
- [ ] Risk reward is more that 1:3
